To Soon, To Young

The steel door is motionless as you begin to hear footsteps click against the concerte floor. As you fumble with another plate, you turn to see the killer wearing a mask. Before you turn toward the door, a loud noise explodes from the shotgun the killer holds at your chest. You are frozen as you look down at your bleeding stomach. You fall to your knees as your clothes are soaked with your blood. The shotgun causes a gaping hole to form in your body. You lean against the steel door as your eyes flutter and your body begins to turn cold.

The killer takes off his mask, revealing stunning golden irises. Before you die you hear him speak.

"It didn't have to be this way. So young, so soon of a death." he says as your vison goes dark.

Fear is weird to feel. The killer only allowed one guess before I died. It was his game I played. He was the puppeteer and I was his puppet.

Age is but a number, you either die TO SOON OR TO YOUNG.

You died.
