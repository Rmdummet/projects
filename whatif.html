What If I Deserve This

You turn right and run towards the light. Confusion sparks across your face then pain rushes through your body as you hear a high pitch sound like a trigger going off. The sledgehammer slides out of your grip as you fall to your knees. You feel stinging against your thigh, you look down at your leg and see an empty syringe. You rip it out of your thigh as you fall to your back. You look up at the sky as the trees begin to blur and move in ways only drugs cause. Leaves crunching under shoes reverberate in your ears and get louder as the killer walks up to you.

He squats down next to you, you see his face. Quite attractive, but sorrow encases his features as he tilts his head slightly. Pain rushes through you as your heart pounds in your chest as if it could explode. Tears stream down your cheeks.

"Why?" you choke out through the pain as your body goes numb.

The man stares at you with his golden orbs as he contemplates what to say.

"Ah, the most important question. There is no answer, it just is. People die all the time, you just happen to be one of them today." he replies almost sad.

Your vison fades as your breath hitchs.

Death is a strange feeling. You feel a mixture of pain, numbness and regret. People say your life flashes before you die. That did not happen for you, you doubted your beliefs. One question rang in your ears several times before you died...

WHAT IF I DESERVED THIS?

You died.

The End.
