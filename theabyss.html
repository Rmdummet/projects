The Abyss, The Blackhole

The second steel door stands unmoving. Before you can react, the floor below you opens and you fall into a unknown pit. You scream out a blood curdling scream as you see metal spikes though your chest, legs and arms. Every breath burns as if you are on fire. Every small motion of your body sends radiating pain to your nervous system. You hear the slow and steady drip of your blood leaving your body and sliding down the metal spikes.

You hear the intercom buzz to life.

"I see you found my little pit of death. It will be nice to see you rot there." The killer's voice states satisfied.

You scream out as the pain overtakes you. Your body sinks deeper into the metal stakes as time passes.

Before you died thoughts surfaced. Memories of those you loved never finding your body, part of you didn't want them to find your mutalated body. The possiblities are endless in the dark...

The never ending ABYSS, THE BLACKHOLE.
