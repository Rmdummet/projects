Waking Up

You wake up lying on top of a disgusting mattress in a lonely dark room. Thin rays of sun enter the room as you try to look through the barred window that is caked with dirt. You sit up as you feel your head throbbing.

You let out a heavy sigh contemplating what to do next. You notice the door is slightly cracked open.

"The only way to make it out alive is to be smart and fight." you think to yourself as you stand up.

Even though your body is badly bruised and your limbs ache, you press forward with your new found determination.
