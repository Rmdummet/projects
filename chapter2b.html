Perception of Time

You shine the light onto the door and read the riddle.

"I am something people love or hate. I change peoples appearances and thoughts. If a person takes care of themself I will go up even higher. To some I will fool them. To others I am a mystery. Some people might want to try and hide me but I will show. No matter how hard people try I will Never go down. What am I?"

You are silent as you figure out the answer. You hope you are correct as you slide the plate into the door.
